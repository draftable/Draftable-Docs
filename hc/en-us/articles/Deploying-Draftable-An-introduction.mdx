Welcome to the Administrator Deployment Track for Draftable Legal. This article provides an introduction to deploying Draftable across your organization and explains how to use this deployment track effectively.

## Who is this deployment track for?

<Note>
This deployment track is intended for **IT administrators, deployment teams, and system administrators** who are responsible for:

- Deploying Draftable Legal to **multiple users** across their organization
- Configuring settings via **Group Policy** or **Registry** to pre-configure Draftable for end users
- Setting up **Document Management System (DMS)** integrations
- Configuring **custom error messaging** and troubleshooting options
- Setting up **advanced workflows** and add-ins for users
</Note>

This track is designed for **organization-wide, large-scale deployments** where centralized configuration and management are required.

---

## Just need a simple single-user installation?

<Warning>
**Not doing an enterprise deployment?** If you're looking to install Draftable Legal for yourself as a single user, you don't need this deployment track. Instead, use the default installer:

<Steps>
<Step title="Download the installer">
Download the Draftable Desktop application here: [https://draftable.com/desktop/download/](https://draftable.com/desktop/download/)
</Step>
<Step title="Run the installer">
Run the installer and follow the prompts.
</Step>
<Step title="Activate your license">
Activate the product by entering your Draftable Legal product key. If you already have a license, your product key should have been sent to your email. If you want to start a trial, [request a demo here](https://www.draftable.com/draftable-legal).
</Step>
<Step title="You're ready!">
That's it! Draftable Legal is now set up and ready to use.
</Step>
</Steps>

For learning how to use Draftable Legal after installation, head to the [Draftable Legal User Academy](/hc/en-us/articles/How-to-use-this-academy).
</Warning>
