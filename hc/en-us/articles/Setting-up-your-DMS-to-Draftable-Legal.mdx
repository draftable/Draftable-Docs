This article provides a comprehensive guide for administrators to configure Document Management System (DMS) integrations with Draftable Legal.

<Note>
**Not deploying a DMS?** Document Management Systems are an alternative document storage and management solution that can be used in conjunction with Draftable Legal. If you are not intending to deploy any DMS integrations for your organization, you can safely skip this article.
</Note>

<Warning>
**Post-installation requirement:** DMS configuration should be completed after Draftable Legal has been installed on user machines. However, some configuration components may have been completed earlier in your deployment process (e.g., the iManage HTTP Listener service). Review each section to determine what steps are still required for your environment.
</Warning>

---
