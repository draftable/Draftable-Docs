<Note>
**Note:**

You must ensure you have the Draftable Word Add-in installed in Microsoft Word to use this feature
</Note>

## Loading email attachments into Draftable Legal

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Open the email containing the file attachments.  
        
        <Frame>
        ![](/images/using-draftable-load-an-attached-file-from-email/image-1.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Navigate to the Draftable Tab within the email window, and then select the **Compare** icon.
        
        <Frame>
        ![](/images/using-draftable-load-an-attached-file-from-email/image-2.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
    You will be prompted to select the files you wish to compare from the email. In this case, we will select the Left and Right PDF files.
        
        <Frame>
        ![](/images/using-draftable-load-an-attached-file-from-email/image-3.png)  
        </Frame>
 
        <Frame>
        ![](/images/using-draftable-load-an-attached-file-from-email/image-4.png)  
        </Frame>
        Once you click **Select** the files will load into the New Comparison Window.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Once the files have loaded, you can run the comparison by selecting theÂ **compare** button.  
        
        <Frame>
        ![](/images/using-draftable-load-an-attached-file-from-email/image-5.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>
