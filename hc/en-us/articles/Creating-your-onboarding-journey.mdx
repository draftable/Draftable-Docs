## Recommended learning paths

### For legal professionals

1. Start with [Redline comparisons](/hc/en-us/articles/19002235946265-Redline-comparisons) - the core of legal document work
2. Learn about [Comparison types](/hc/en-us/articles/33190528521881-Comparison-Type-How-to-choose-the-right-option-in-the-Draftable-New-Comparison-Window)
3. Set up your [1-Click Compare workflow](/hc/en-us/articles/31611819497753-Using-1-Click-Compare-to-run-comparisons-with-your-preferred-default-workflow)

### For power users

1. Master [Bulk Compare](/hc/en-us/articles/30062521598361-Bulk-Compare-How-to-load-and-run-multiple-comparisons-at-once) for handling multiple documents
2. Explore [Three-Pane View](/hc/en-us/articles/31975077731225-Three-Pane-View-See-changes-in-context-with-older-newer-and-redlined-documents-in-one-screen)
3. Learn [Excel](/hc/en-us/articles/19002208522393-Comparing-Excel-files-in-Draftable-Legal) and [PowerPoint](/hc/en-us/articles/32800088338073-Comparing-PowerPoint-files-in-Draftable-Legal) comparison
