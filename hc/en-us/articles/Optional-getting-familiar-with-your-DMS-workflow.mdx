**Not using a DMS?** If you do not intend to use a Document Management System (DMS) in your workflow, you can safely skip this article as part of your onboarding.
</Warning>

## Introduction

If you are using a Document Management System (DMS) with Draftable Legal, you should be aware that the workflows are slightly different when:

- **Selecting files** from your DMS to load into a comparison
- **Saving exports or comparisons** directly back to your DMS

This article provides links to the relevant DMS-specific guides so you can learn the workflow that applies to your organization.
