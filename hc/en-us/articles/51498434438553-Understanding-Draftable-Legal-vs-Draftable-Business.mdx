## Draftable Legal

**Draftable Legal** is built specifically for legal professionals and teams who need fast, reliable document comparison with advanced tools for redlining and document management system (DMS) integrations.

### Key features

- Designed for **legal workflows** with Redline comparisons and markup control
- Integrates with leading DMS solutions such as **iManage**, **NetDocuments**, **SharePoint**, and **Worldox**
- Advanced **comparison options** including three-pane view, Change List reports, tagging, and notes
- Customization via **Group Policy** and **enterprise deployment support**
- Enhanced **security and compliance** features for law firms and corporate legal teams

### Pricing

Draftable Legal is available via an **annual subscription** with pricing tailored to your firm’s size and deployment requirements.  
To receive a custom quote or request a demo, visit the [**Draftable Legal Pricing Page**](https://www.draftable.com/pricing).

**Licensing options:**

- **Single-user licenses** for individual professionals
- **Team licenses** for small to medium law firms
- **Enterprise licenses** with centralized management, DMS integrations, and volume discounts

For enterprise environments, Draftable Legal can also be deployed using **machine-wide or per-user MSI installers** and managed centrally via **Group Policy** or the **Windows registry** (see [Enterprise Deployment Options](/hc/en-us/articles/18763487262105-Configuring-settings-with-Group-Policy-Draftable-Legal)).

## Draftable Business

**Draftable Business** is designed for general business users and enterprise teams that need powerful, accurate document comparison without the specialized legal features of Draftable Legal.

### Key features

- Compare **Word, PDF, PowerPoint, and Excel** files with precision
- Side-by-side and overlay (Redline) comparison modes
- Easy-to-use interface for professionals across all industries
- Integrates with local and cloud storage solutions
- Available for both **Windows and macOS**

### Pricing

Draftable Business is offered as a **per-user annual subscription**.  
You can view the latest pricing or start a free trial from the [**Draftable Business Pricing Page**](https://www.draftable.com/pricing).

**Licensing options:**

- **Business (Single-User)** – Ideal for individual professionals or small teams
- **Business (Enterprise)** – Includes centralized deployment, priority support, and volume-based pricing

## Comparing Draftable Legal and Draftable Business

| Feature | Draftable Legal | Draftable Business |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Designed for | Legal professionals | General business users |
| Comparison modes | Redline, Side-by-Side, Track Changes | Side-by-Side |
| DMS integrations | iManage, NetDocuments, SharePoint, Worldox | N/A |
| Enterprise deployment | Yes (MSI, Group Policy, Intune support) | Yes (MSI installers) |
| Add-ins | Word & Outlook, ExcelA dd-ins, Redline in Email | Word Add-in, Outlook |
| Customization & profiles | Redline Profiles, Group Policy | Application-level settings |

## How to Get Started

1. Visit the [**Draftable Pricing Page**](https://www.draftable.com/pricing).
2. Choose between **Draftable Business** or **Draftable Legal** based on your needs.
3. Request a **demo** or **custom quote** for your organization.
4. Download and install your selected version of Draftable to begin your free trial.
