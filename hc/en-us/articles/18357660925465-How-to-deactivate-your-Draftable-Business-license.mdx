## Deactivating your license

<Steps>
<Step>
Right click the Draftable Desktop icon in the toolbar and select **settings.** 

<Frame>
![](/images/draftable-business/image-11-1.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step>
In the **licensing** tab, select **deactivate,** and in the following screen click **yes.** 

<Frame>
![](/images/draftable-business/image-11.png)
</Frame>
<br/>
<Frame>
![](/images/draftable-business/image-12.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step>
This will remove your product key from your Draftable Business application, which will allow you to enter a new product key in the following screen below.  
	  
If you don't intend on using a new key, you can simply close this window.  
	   
<Frame>
![](/images/draftable-business/image-13.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step>
It is important to contact the Draftable support team at [support@draftable.com](mailto:support@draftable.com) when you do deactivate licenses if you intend in using those licenses on other machines.
</Step>
</Steps>
