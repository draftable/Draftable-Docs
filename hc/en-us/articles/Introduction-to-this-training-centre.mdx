Welcome to the **Training Centre & Video Resources**, your destination for hands-on, video-based training content for Draftable Legal, as well as training management information and supplementary resources.

<Warning>
**Stop! Are you an individual user looking to learn Draftable Legal?**

If you're here to learn Draftable Legal for yourself through structured, self-paced learning, **this section isn't your best starting point**.

We recommend starting with the [Draftable Legal User Academy](/hc/en-us/articles/How-to-use-this-academy) instead. It provides comprehensive written guides and step-by-step learning paths designed for individual users.

This Training Centre is an **alternative and supplementary resource**, particularly useful if you prefer learning through video content or if you're responsible for training others.
</Warning>

## Who should use this Training Centre?

This Training Centre is designed for:

- **Visual learners** who prefer watching video content over reading documentation
- **Training managers and trainers** who need to deliver Draftable training to their teams
- **Anyone looking for supplementary resources** to complement their learning from the User Academy

<Note>
**A note for visual learners:** Not all Draftable Legal content has been made into video format. Please be aware that only some topics have video tutorials available. For comprehensive coverage of all features, the [Draftable Legal User Academy](/hc/en-us/articles/How-to-use-this-academy) remains the most complete resource.
</Note>
