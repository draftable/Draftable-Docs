title: "Draftable in Office"
description: "Bringing Office and Draftable closer together for more efficient workflows."
---

**Draftable in Office** is a modern Word add-in that brings Draftable’s powerful document comparison functionality directly into Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint. \
This feature enables users to perform comparisons, manage versions, and review results — all within familiar Office interface.

> **_Note:_**_ Draftable in Office requires _**_Draftable version 25.10.000 or later_**_ to be installed and running on the user’s system._
>
> _This article only shows the workflow for Microsoft Word at this time. _

Once installed, the add-in appears under the **Home** tab in Microsoft Word.
